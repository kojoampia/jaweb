Career Paths

At Jojo Addison Consultancy, each person is either a consultant, an associate consultant or on the path to becoming a consultant.
As a consultant trainee, you’re expected to take on a project from inception to completion under the guidance of a senior consultant.
As an associate consultant, you’re guided by a senior consultant in your project delivery. You’re also expected as part of your consultancy tasks to provide tutelage and guidance to consultant trainees.
As a consultant or senior consultant, you’ll be expected to independently carry out each project under your supervision from inception to completion.
Each consultant is required to wear different hats at all times.

  • Business Requirement Analysis
  • Technical Requirement Analysis
  • Acceptance Criteria Analysis
  • Programming deliverables
  • Test Requirement Analysis
  • Documentation and tutorial writing
  • Team, client presentation and publication

We strive to provide you with all the necessary tools and resources to make your journey as smooth as possible.
